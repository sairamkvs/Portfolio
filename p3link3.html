Project Overview

This project aims to provide a smart and efficient solution for monitoring and managing water levels in tanks remotely. The system utilizes wireless sensors to transmit water level data, ensuring optimal water usage and preventing overflow or shortages.

Key Features

  • Wireless communication for remote monitoring of water levels.
  • Automated controls to maintain optimal water levels, preventing wastage.
  • Alerts and notifications for maintenance and usage efficiency.

Technologies Used

  • Arduino IDE: For coding and controlling sensors.
  • Wireless Sensors: To monitor water levels and transmit data.
  • Web Interface: For real-time monitoring and control.
