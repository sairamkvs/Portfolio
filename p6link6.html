Project Overview

The Digital Hotel Menu project aims to enhance the guest experience in hotels by replacing traditional paper menus with a digital, interactive, and visually appealing interface. Designed using UI/UX principles and created with Figma, the project focuses on providing an intuitive, engaging, and modern solution for hotel dining services.

Key Features

  • Interactive and user-friendly interface with touch support.
  • Dynamic updates for menu items and pricing without the need for reprints.
  • Multiple language support to cater to international guests.
  • High-quality images and descriptions of dishes to enhance the visual appeal.
  • Special promotions and recommendations based on time of day or season.
  • Feedback and rating system integrated for guest satisfaction tracking.

Technologies Used

  • Figma: Used for designing the interactive UI/UX of the digital menu.
  • HTML & CSS: For structuring and styling the web application interface.
  • JavaScript: For adding interactivity and dynamic content updates.
  • Responsive Design: Ensures compatibility across various devices, including tablets and smartphones.
