Project Overview

The Inclined Surface Climbing Vehicle is designed to clean both inclined and flat surfaces effectively. It utilizes specialized mechanisms to maintain stability and grip, making it suitable for tasks such as window cleaning, solar panel maintenance, and industrial applications.

Key Features

  • Ability to climb inclined surfaces up to a specific angle with stability.
  • Equipped with cleaning mechanisms for surfaces like windows and solar panels.
  • Remote control operation for safety and ease of use.

Technologies Used

  • Mechanical Design: Custom-built chassis and climbing mechanisms.
  • Arduino: For controlling motors and sensors.
  • Remote Control: For wireless operation and maneuverability.
